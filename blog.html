What's poppin' at PAWS?

2020-2021

December 2020

12/14/2020

Hey y'all! Today we had a quick check in with our group leaders over current projects. Please contact us if you still do not know your group! Fleece blanket drop-offs are this Saturday and Sunday from 3 - 4pm at Westview. We will not have another meeting until after Winter Break, so we wish everyone a happy and safe holiday break!

November 2020

11/30/2020

Greetings! In today's meeting, we split into groups and started working on the various volunteer opportunities. If you missed today's meeting or don't know what group you're in, feel free to email us and we'll get you caught up! Fleece pickup is this weekend, 12/5 and 12/6 from 3 - 4pm at Westview. Our next meeting will be 12/14, don't forget to sign up for Amazon Smiles to support Indigo Rescue, and we'll see y'all soon!

11/16/2020

Hello everybody! Today, we briefly went over cat grass drop off and new volunteer opportunities - Amazon Smiles, Indigo Rescue Instagram and posters, PAWS Instagram posts, and sticker designs. Our next meeting will be 11/30, which will count for volunteer hours!

11/2/2020

Thanks to everyone who showed up to our first meeting of the school year! We went over what PAWS does as a club, introduced the board members, and announced our first project: growing cat grass! More information coming soon in an email. Our next meeting will be 11/16, in two weeks. See y'all then!

September 2020

9/16/2020

Hey y'all! In the midst of these confusing times, we want to be able to meet and connect with you, as hard as it is! We'll be starting up meetings online again shortly, and we hope to see you there!
